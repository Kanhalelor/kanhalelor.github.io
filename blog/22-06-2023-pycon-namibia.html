Reflecting on PyCon Namibia 2023.

Robson - 22-06-2023 | #python
Me at Pycon Namibia - 2023

Hello Pythonistas!

. . I love Python 😉 . .

In today's rapidly evolving technosphere, there has never been a better time to immerse oneself in the world of technology, learning, building, and sharing experiences with fellow experts. The globalized nature of technical events enables us to connect and collaborate with professionals across various domains, identify knowledge gaps, and cultivate our skills with newfound expertise.

The Power of Python:

No discussion about PyCon Namibia is complete without acknowledging the incredible impact of Python. This versatile and captivating language has taken the web by storm, becoming the go-to tool for nearly every developer.

Day 1: A Thrilling Experience:

I had the privilege of delving into the realms of Machine Learning and Data Science alongside my fellow speaker and newfound friend, Olaniyan Adewale Hafeez. Our exploration offered fresh perspectives and profound insights. Moreover, Adewale delivered an engaging demo on Microcontrollers and circuit programming using Micropython—an efficient implementation of Python tailored for small embedded development boards. This demonstration was invaluable, particularly for the enthusiastic audience of learners present at the conference.

Day 2: Enlightening Talks and Knowledge-Sharing:

On the second day, I stumbled upon an enlightening talk by Natu Lauchande titled "Introduction to machine learning engineering with MLflow." This session revealed the remarkable features of MLflow, which proved indispensable in fine-tuning Machine Learning workflows. Additionally, the day featured numerous productive workshops on Data Science and Technical documentation, enriching attendees with practical skills and insights.

The Grand Finale: Inspiring Talks and Unforgettable Moments:

The final day of PyCon NA 2023 presented an opportunity for me to deliver a practical demo on building Real-time Machine Learning Data Applications using Python. It was a momentous occasion as I stepped onto the stage for my first demo at PyCon NA. The day unfolded with a captivating series of talks by esteemed international speakers, including Juan Luis Cano Rodríguez , and John Sandall. The event culminated in lightning talks, adding a delightful touch and leaving participants inspired and motivated.

Looking Ahead to PyCon NA 2024:

As we set our sights on PyCon NA 2024, it is vital to foster increased stakeholder engagement and garner support from educational institutions, including schools and tertiary organizations, as well as private sector involvement. PyCon Namibia truly belongs to us, the Namibians, and by embracing these collaborations, we can further elevate the event's impact and reach.

Kudos to the PyCon NA 2023 organizing team for successfully pulling off this remarkable event.

Enjoy the Gallery, and see you at PyCon NA 2024!
