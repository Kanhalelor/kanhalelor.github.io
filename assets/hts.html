Windhoek Technical High School is the only technical school in the Khomas Region of Namibia, offering Technical and Science subjects to learners in grades 8-12. As a newly graduated computer science teacher at the school, I am deeply passionate about providing quality education to our students.

However, we currently face a significant challenge—the lack of a fully functional computer lab. This hinders our ability to deliver effective instruction and deprives our students of vital hands-on experience in computer science and design and technology.
